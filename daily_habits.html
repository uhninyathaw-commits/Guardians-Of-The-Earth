Simple Daily habits

  • Plant trees or flowers at home
  • Water plants regularly
  • Do not litter in parks or streets
  • Recycle paper, plastic, and bottles
  • Use reusable bags and bottles
  • Walk or ride a bicycle instead of driving
  • Save water when gardening
  • Join community clean-up activities
  • Protect trees and avoid damaging plants
  • Spend time in parks and appreciate nature
  • Turn off lights and save electricity
  • Teach others about the importance of green spaces
