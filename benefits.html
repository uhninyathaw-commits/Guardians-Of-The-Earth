Benefits Of Green Spaces

Improves air quality Plants absorb carbon dioxide and release oxygen, helping reduce air pollution. Reduces heat Trees and plants provide shade and cool the surrounding area, lowering urban temperatures. Supports mental health Green spaces help reduce stress, anxiety, and improve mood and relaxation. Encourages physical activity Parks and gardens provide places for walking, exercising, and recreational activities. Controls flooding and rainwater Soil and plants absorb rainwater, reducing water runoff and flood risks. Supports biodiversity Green areas provide habitats for birds, insects, and other wildlife. Reduces noise pollution Trees and shrubs can block or absorb noise from traffic and crowded areas. Improves community life Public green spaces encourage social interaction and make cities more attractive. Helps fight climate change Plants store carbon and help reduce greenhouse gases in the atmosphere. Increases aesthetic value Green environments make places look cleaner, healthier, and more beautiful.
